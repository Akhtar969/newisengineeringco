How to Get the Fastest Quote

Include these details in your enquiry for M.S. Flanges, E Table Flanges, Pipes or Fittings and receive a complete, accurate quotation within 24 hours.

1. Product Type

Specify the exact product: M.S. Flange, E Table Flange (IS:1538), Blind Flange, Welding Neck Flange, Socket Weld Flange, Elbow (45°/90°), Reducer (Concentric/Eccentric), Square Pipe, Round Pipe, etc.

2. Size (Nominal Bore)

State the size in NB (Nominal Bore) — e.g., 15mm NB, 50mm NB, 100mm NB, 150mm NB, 200mm NB, etc. For pipes, state the size and wall thickness or weight series (Light/Medium/Heavy).

3. Quantity Required

Mention the quantity in numbers (nos.) for flanges and fittings, or in metres/tonnes for pipes. Bulk orders receive special discount pricing. Minimum order quantities may apply for custom sizes.

4. Standard & Rating

For flanges, specify the standard required: IS:1538 Table E (E Table), ANSI B16.5, IS, BS. Pressure rating: PN6, PN10, PN16 or Class 150/300/600. Face type: Flat Face (FF), Raised Face (RF).

5. Delivery Location & Timeline

State your delivery city or project site location (Mumbai, Pune, Delhi, Bangalore, etc.) and required delivery date if urgent. This helps us give you accurate freight costs and delivery timelines.

6. Special Requirements

Mention any special requirements: Material Test Certificates (MTC), third-party inspection, specific surface finish, painting / galvanizing, custom bore dimensions or non-standard specifications. We accommodate project-specific needs.
