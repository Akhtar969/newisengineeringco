What are E Table Flanges and why are they used?

+

E Table Flanges are flanges manufactured as per Indian Standard IS:1538 Table E. They are the mandatory standard for Indian municipal water supply, sewage treatment, fire-fighting and public sector piping projects. Available in sizes 15mm NB to 600mm NB in pressure ratings PN6, PN10 and PN16 with flat face (FF) design. I.S. Engineering Co. is a specialist E Table Flanges manufacturer in Mumbai since 1999.
